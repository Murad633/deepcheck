About Project:

Idea

In the system we offer, with the help of AI the plant in the pot is identified and the temperature and humidity are regulated according to plant. Watering times of plants are changing depending on the degree of humidity, temperature and storage conditions of the flower. Our system waters flowers when they need.The monitor on the pot informs the user about the condition of the plant. Also, user can get real time information about the plant through the application on the phone.

Problems

1. People who are going on vacation are not able to water plants while they are on holidays

2. People with a heavy work schedule are not able to care for their plants, flowers.

3. Sometimes people who love flowers keep the flower in a very dry place and the leaves fade even if they are watered by default watering time.

4. Root system damaged from being kept too wet

Purpose

Our goal is to help people who go on vacation or don’t have much time caring for flowers because of work, but want to take care of their plants. The system we offer always takes care of their plants and does not allow their leaves to wither and their roots to be damaged. Also, they can always get information about the condition of their plants remotely when they are away from home.

Competitive Advantages

80-90% of self-watering pots that are sold in stores and online stores are not electronic. Even the electronic ones simply water the pot by starting an engine for a while. Watering times of plants are changing depending on the degree of humidity, temperature and storage conditions of the flower. But the pot that we offer are completely solar powered electronic system. With the help of Aİ, the ideal soil temperature and humidity for each plant is regulated. Water is poured in when the plant needs it, and are displayed on a monitor on the pot when needed. Also, users can get current and up to date information about plant via application in the phone.
